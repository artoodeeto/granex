About Us

Granex Industrial Engineering Services, was established on January 13, 2022, located in the southern part of Mindanao in the city of General Santos, South Cotabato. A sole proprietorship, owned and manage by Paul Joshua N. Gako.

Our Marketing Director, Maria Luisa N. Gako has been in this field for more than 30 years, thus cementing her expertise in the supply of Industrial Parts.

We sourced out our supplies not only domestically but globally as well. We get our parts and other supplies in the United States, Europe and Asia. We only sourced out products from reliable suppliers which Quality is their main focus.

We believe in our capability and capacity to serve our clients requirements, not only with our quality products but with our sales and technical people who are assigned in the area for easy access of inquiries and services we offer.

Our Mission

To service our clientele with honesty, integrity and with quality products. Provide technical and after sales service to our clients.

We will strive to make a long-term relationship with our clients based on safety, quality, & timely service in anticipation of their requirements.

In order for us to achieve our goals in our mission, we will treat our staff with respect and involve them in the quality improvement of our services to ensure responsiveness and effective work execution.

Our Vision

To empower and inspire more people to be independent and to look forward to a better future by working hard with integrity, sincerity, honesty and loyalty in order to achieve our goals and build a strong relationship and partnership with one another.
